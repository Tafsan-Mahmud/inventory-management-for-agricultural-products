Common Questions

FAQ

Is Modern Agro free to use?

Yes, the dashboard is completely free for individual farmers. Pro plans unlock multi-farm and team features.

Where is my data stored?

Your inventory data is stored locally in your browser. Pro accounts get optional encrypted cloud sync.

Does it work offline?

Absolutely. Once loaded, the dashboard works fully offline — perfect for fields without reliable internet.

Can I export my inventory data?

Yes. Export to CSV, Excel, or PDF directly from the Reports page in your dashboard.
