About Us

Rooted in the land.
Grown with technology.

We believe farming deserves tools as modern as any Fortune-500 office. Modern Agro is our answer.

Our Story

Modern Agro started on a rainy afternoon in 2021, when our founder watched his grandfather track an entire season's harvest in a soggy notebook. We knew there had to be a better way.

Today, thousands of farms across South Asia and beyond use Modern Agro to manage millions of kilograms of produce — without ever getting a page wet.
